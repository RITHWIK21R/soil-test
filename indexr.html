    Soil Parameter Explanations

    Soil pH: Indicates the acidity or alkalinity of the soil. Ideal pH for most crops is 6.0-7.5.

    Nitrogen (N): Essential for plant growth and leafy development.

    Phosphorus (P): Important for root development and flowering.

    Potassium (K): Enhances disease resistance and improves fruit quality.

    Soil Texture: Refers to the proportion of sand, silt, and clay in the soil.

    Moisture Content: Measures the water content in the soil, critical for proper irrigation.

    Calcium (Ca): Strengthens plant cell walls and promotes root development.

    Magnesium (Mg): Essential for photosynthesis and enzyme activity.
